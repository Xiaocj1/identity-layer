## Chapter 2: A Vision, Not a Blueprint

### 2.1 What If?

What if your devices actually knew you?

- Switch contexts, and your phone understands who you are right now.
- Get a new phone? Log in once. Everything is there.
- Have thoughts you don't want to share? A space that's truly yours.
- Your digital selfâ€”yours. Not owned by any company. Not locked into any ecosystem.

### 2.2 Why the Protocol Layer?

If every app builds its own identity system:

- WeChat has one
- TikTok has one
- Taobao has one
- Users manage countless accounts
- Developers rebuild the same thing over and over

If it lives at the protocol layer:

- Define it once. Every app can use it.
- One identifier works everywhere.
- Build once. Everyone benefits.

Application-layer identity is an **account**.  
Protocol-layer identity is **you**.

Accounts multiply. You don't.
